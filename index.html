About Me

I enjoying coding, making things work, and making web pages and web apps.

I'm a former Early Years Educator turned Front-End Developer — a career pivot that’s driven by the same passion: creating meaningful, human-centered experiences.

After over a decade helping young minds grow, I now channel that same empathy, patience, and problem-solving into designing and building responsive, accessible websites and apps.

I’m particularly drawn to clean UI, intuitive design, and the satisfaction of bringing ideas to life in the browser. My toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React — with each project in my portfolio representing a new skill learned or challenge overcome.

What excites me most about development is that it’s never static. There’s always more to build, test, and improve — and I thrive in that loop. Right now, I’m seeking a junior dev role where I can continue growing, contribute to real-world projects, and learn from experienced engineers.
