About Me

I enjoy coding, solving problems, and bringing websites and web apps to life in the browser.

I’m a front-end developer with full-stack capabilities and a strong foundation in user-centered design, accessibility, and responsive interfaces. After over a decade helping young minds grow as an early childhood educator, I now bring that same empathy, patience, and drive into crafting intuitive digital experiences.

I’m particularly drawn to clean UI, intuitive UX, and the satisfaction of transforming ideas into working applications. My core toolkit includes HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React — paired with backend experience using Node.js, Express, REST APIs, and working knowledge of PostgreSQL.

I thrive on building end-to-end solutions, from the interface to the underlying logic and data. I’m actively seeking opportunities to contribute to meaningful products, grow with a supportive team, and continue building software that makes a difference.
